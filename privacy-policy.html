Code Blue Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Code Blue Android application ("Code Blue," "the app," "we," or "our") handles information.

Code Blue is a clinical documentation and quick-reference aid. It is not an electronic medical record, emergency dispatch service, medical device, or substitute for professional judgment, training, or institutional protocols.

Information entered into the app

Users may record Code Blue event times, rhythms, shocks, medications, procedures, notes, and other event details. This information may concern a patient and may be sensitive health information.

Code Blue stores these event records locally on the user's device. We do not operate a server that receives, stores, or has access to these event records. Users should avoid entering patient names, medical-record numbers, dates of birth, or other direct identifiers unless authorized by their institution and applicable law.

Sharing and deletion

The app shares an event report only when the user selects the share function and chooses another application or recipient. The selected third-party service handles that shared information under its own privacy practices.

Event history remains on the device until the app's storage is cleared or the app is uninstalled. Device backups may retain app data depending on the user's Android and backup settings.

Advertising and device data

Code Blue uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements when an active Code Blue event is not in progress. Google Mobile Ads may automatically collect or process information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers and other device identifiers;
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it;
  • Device, operating-system, app, and network information;
  • Ad impressions, clicks, interactions, diagnostics, and performance data.

Google may use this information to provide, measure, secure, and personalize advertising, subject to user choices, regional consent requirements, and Google's policies. Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and Google's advertising information.

Permissions

  • Internet and network state: used to load advertisements.
  • Notifications: used for CPR-cycle and medication reminders.
  • Vibration: used for time-sensitive reminders.

Data security

Local records are protected by the security controls of the user's Android device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Users and healthcare organizations are responsible for device access, institutional authorization, retention requirements, and compliance with applicable privacy and health-information laws.

Children

Code Blue is intended for trained adult healthcare personnel and is not directed to children.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app's practices or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the latest revision.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information displayed on the Code Blue listing in Google Play.
